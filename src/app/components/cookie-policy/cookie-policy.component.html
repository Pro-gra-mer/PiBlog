1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored in your browser when you visit websites. They help improve your experience, remember your preferences, and display personalized content.

2. Types of cookies we use

a. Technical cookies

These are essential for the proper functioning of the website. They do not collect personal data and do not require your consent.

b. Analytics cookies

We use Google Analytics and Search Console to understand how users navigate the site anonymously and to improve its performance.

More information available at the Google Privacy Policy.

c. Third-party cookies

Some embedded content (such as videos or images hosted on YouTube or Cloudinary) may use cookies from those external services.
